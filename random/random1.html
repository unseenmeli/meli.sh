Within every joke there is truth

Lies are like hives, no matter how they are, there's a reason they are there. In Georgia there is this saying (I actually do not know if there is, might just be my saying) fuck the joke if there is not a little bit of truth in it... sounds better in Georgian, but you get the point! Whether it is subconscious or not, we all put a different kind of meaning in the joke-- well not all the times but yeah-- not just making someone laugh. Or at least I like to think so, yet I find it beautiful... sometimes humans cannot overcome the stage of being forward, so we create these random paths of trying to get to the result.

Yet this word "Result" rounds everything up, doesn't it? We gave it the meaning of "Point", for example: why are you writing this random article/diary (or if we rephrase it), what is the point? The whole idea of meli-- me-- is doing stuff without having a point-- of course I am not implying to do reckless stuff, but I do not wish to have to think why I do everything I do, you know? I do this because this is me.

Anyways, back to the topic! Result that rounds everything up when it should not. I mean, of course result is meaningful, we need to achieve something when doing something, but how we achieve it should be the lovely part of it! I don't want to sit here and talk about anything I achieved without talking about how I achieved them! The story is the main dish, if you may!

SOOOO what did this idea give you? That result is bad?-- absolutely not, because it gives us history! I HAVE THIS RESULT BECAUSE I DID THIS! The most meaningful thing a human can say in my opinion! I did say this, let's get to topic, but I never really did, so shall we?

I find it amazing how people try to get around stuff (creating a path to tell what you really feel through lying). I MEAN ISN'T THAT AMAZING?? Expressing the most YOU kind of feeling while covering it up with the most NOT YOU kind of vibe? Delightful! That is why I love humanity-- a human is a social construct-- no wonder we are so good at it! We go through this everyday and we keep sharpening the blade we sharpened millions of times!
