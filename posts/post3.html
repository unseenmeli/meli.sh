How I transcribed my grandmother's book using AI

December 2024

So my grandmother, Manana Parunashvili, wrote a book called "House of Daisies" which is essentially a book about her life and experiences. This book is written in Georgian and so we came across an issue. We do not know which AI could transcribe Georgian text. First instinct was claude/chatgpt. So let's see what they can do even now ( I started this project like 7 months ago, I am lazy what can I say ).
